Our Story

Who We Are

Founded in 2010, ExploreEra began as a small travel agency with a big dream - to make world-class travel experiences accessible to everyone. What started as a passion project between three travel enthusiasts has now grown into one of India's most trusted travel companies.

We believe that travel has the power to transform lives, broaden perspectives, and create unforgettable memories. Our mission is to craft personalized journeys that go beyond typical tourism, offering authentic experiences that connect you with the heart and soul of each destination.

Our Approach

At ExploreEra, we don't just sell trips - we create experiences. Each itinerary is carefully designed by our team of destination experts who have personally visited and vetted every location we recommend.

We work directly with local guides and operators to ensure authentic experiences while maintaining the highest standards of safety and comfort. Our partnerships allow us to offer exclusive access and unique opportunities you won't find elsewhere.
